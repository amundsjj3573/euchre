Our members

Jonah Amundsen

Jonah Amundsen is a senior at UW-Eau Claire, He is a math major with a computer science minor. He is from Rochester, Minnesota. The first time that he played Euchre was in June of 2019. After he graduates from UWEC in May, he will be attending Michigan State University in their math PhD program starting in the fall.

Holly McFarlane

Holly McFarlane is a second year English, Critical Studies major with a minor in Women’s, Gender, and Sexuality Studies at UW-Eau Claire. She is one of the founding members of the UWEC Euchre Club. Holly grew up playing euchre with her family and it has been a joy to help teach how to play and share her love of the game with others! Her favorite part of Euchre Club is being able to spend time and create new memories with friends. One of her favorite memories from the 2019-2020 season was playing euchre on the cruise ship during the Blugold Marching Band’s 2020 trip to Australia and New Zealand.
